VeriCircuit Tutor

Professor-facing MVP: natural language is parsed into Circuit IR, solved with MNA, verified with circuit-law checks, then explained from the verified Solution Packet. The parser may use an LLM; the final numbers do not come directly from an LLM.
Source of truth: Circuit IR validation, MNA solving, and deterministic verification. Tutor text is generated only after verified values exist.

Status Meaning

PASS
Circuit IR validated, MNA solver ran, deterministic verification passed.
AMBIGUOUS
Parser or validator found unresolved ambiguity. No numerical answer is shown; clarify topology, component directions, or missing ground/reference.
UNSUPPORTED
Problem contains unsupported components or analysis types, such as general transient analysis, DC inductors, diodes, transistors, nonlinear devices, or nonideal op-amp behavior. No numerical answer is shown.
FAIL
The circuit was parsed, but validation, solving, or verification failed.
