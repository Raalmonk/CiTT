\section{Conclusion}

CiTT demonstrates a MATLAB-native path for grounding AI circuit tutoring in executable Simscape/Simulink artifacts. Across three biomedical circuit-learning benchmarks, the system produced live evidence that connects prompts, models, teaching, highlights, probes, plots, and limitations. The project does not claim patient use or exhaustive evaluation. Its contribution is a course/design-competition system showing that language tutoring can be made more inspectable when paired with mature engineering simulation and evidence export.
